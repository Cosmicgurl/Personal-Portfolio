About

Hi, I’m Natalie, a junior front-end developer from Munich. With a background in the glasses industry, I've developed a strong eye for design, attention to detail and an understanding of how aesthetics and functionality come together. These skills have naturally translated into my passion for creating beautiful, user-friendly websites that are both visually appealing and intuitive to navigate.

Although I'm still early in my development journey, I’m committed to honing my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, constantly learning and experimenting with new techniques. This portfolio showcases the projects I’m most proud of so far, as I continue to grow as a developer.

I believe great design goes beyond just looking good—it should create a seamless user experience. Whether it’s a responsive layout, interactive elements, or a clean user interface, my goal is to deliver websites that are as functional as they are aesthetically pleasing.
